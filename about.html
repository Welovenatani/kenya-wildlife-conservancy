Our Mission

We are dedicated to conserving the unique wildlife of Kenya through community engagement, education, and sustainable practices.

Our History

Founded in 2000, the Kenya Wildlife Conservancy was established to address the challenges faced by wildlife and habitats in Kenya. Over the years, we have developed various programs aimed at protecting endangered species and promoting biodiversity.
